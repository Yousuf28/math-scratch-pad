# Elementary Math ScratchPad

A fun and educational iOS app that helps users practice addition, subtraction, and multiplication skills through interactive challenges.

## Features

- **Three Game Modes**: 
  - **Addition (+)**: Classic addition practice
  - **Subtraction (-)**: Practice subtraction with smart number generation (Top >= Bottom)
  - **Multiplication (×)**: Master multiplication with difficulty-tuned ranges
- **Three Difficulty Levels**:
  - **1 Digit**: Numbers 1-9
  - **2 Digits**: 
    - Add/Sub: 10-99
    - Mult: 10-20 × 1-9
  - **3 Digits**:
    - Add/Sub: 100-999
    - Mult: 100-199 × 1-9
- **Interactive Scratch Pad**: Draw and calculate directly on a built-in canvas. This feature helps reduce cognitive load by allowing you to take notes, draw tally marks, and solve problems visually.
- **Reward System**: Earn points for correct answers and unlock difficulties
- **Persistent Scoring**: Your points are saved locally and persist across app launches
- **Audio Feedback**: Success sounds for correct answers
- **Clean UI**: Modern, user-friendly interface designed for focus

## Why the Scratchpad Matters
Math can be overwhelming when you try to hold too many numbers in your head. Our built-in scratchpad allows you to:
- Take notes while counting
- Draw visual aids
- "Carry the one" without losing your place
- Focus on the logic without striving to remember every number
