# Privacy Policy for Elementary Math ScratchPad

**Effective Date: February 3, 2026**

## Overview

Your data belongs to you. Elementary Math ScratchPad acts entirely as a local tool on your device. The Application does not collect, store, or share your personal information or usage data with any third parties.

## Data Collection

The Application does not collect any user data.

- **No Analytics**: The App does not track how you use it.
- **No Account Required**: You do not need to create an account to use the application.
- **No Remote Processing**: All math problems, scoring, and scratchpad drawings are processed locally on your device. Your data never leaves your device.

## Data Handling

- **Scores and Progress**: Your points are stored securely in your device's local storage solely to persist your progress across sessions.
- **ScratchPad Drawings**: Any drawings made on the scratchpad exist only in the device's temporary memory while you are using the specific challenge. They are not saved to storage or transmitted anywhere.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
